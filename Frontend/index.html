How it works

From raw data to
clear explanations

01

Upload your dataset

Drop any CSV file. Instant schema detection, type inference, and preprocessing.

02

Detect anomalies

Choose from multiple detection approaches and toggle between them to compare results.

03

Explore interactively

Time-series and scatter plots with anomalies highlighted. Click any point to drill down.

04

Understand why

SHAP waterfall chart per flagged point. See which features pushed the anomaly score up or down.
